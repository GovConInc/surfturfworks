Common Questions

How quickly can you start service?

For routine maintenance, we can typically begin within one week of signing. Emergency services and green-to-clean pool recovery can often be scheduled within 24-48 hours.

Do you offer service contracts?

Yes, we offer flexible service agreements for both pool and landscaping maintenance. We also work on a month-to-month basis for clients who prefer that arrangement. Commercial and HOA clients typically work under annual contracts.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Surf & Turf LLC is fully licensed to operate in the State of Florida and carries comprehensive commercial liability insurance. We're happy to provide documentation upon request.

Can you handle both my pool and landscaping?

That's our specialty. Many clients appreciate having one reliable company handle all their outdoor property needs. We coordinate pool service and lawn care schedules to minimize disruption and ensure your property looks its best.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Pasco, Hernando, and Pinellas counties, including New Port Richey, Spring Hill, Wesley Chapel, Trinity, Tarpon Springs, and surrounding communities.
