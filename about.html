Our Story

Brad's Electrical Construction Company was established in 2018 by Bradley Oast in Winston Salem, North Carolina, with a clear mission: to provide quality facilities maintenance and repair while creating exceptional working conditions that improve morale and make the company an employer of choice in the industry.

What started as a small company has grown into a trusted electrical contractor serving Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and major universities across North Carolina. With over 20 skilled professionals and decades of combined experience, we've completed projects valued at over $5 million while maintaining our commitment to exceptional customer service.

Today, Brad's Electrical is proud to be a Woman-Owned Small Business, SBA-Certified Small Disadvantaged Business, and GSA Schedule holder, opening doors to serve federal agencies while continuing our tradition of excellence in the commercial sector.
