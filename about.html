Who We Are

Built on Experience.
Driven by Excellence.

Brad's Electrical Construction Company is a certified Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) serving commercial, industrial, and government clients across North Carolina.

What began as a vision to combine high-level management with master-level craftsmanship has grown into one of the region's most trusted electrical contractors. From complex government facilities to large-scale industrial plants, we bring the same level of dedication and detail to every connection we make.

Leadership

Executive Leadership

The synergy of administrative vision and technical mastery.

B

Brooke Oast

President & Owner

As the President of Brad's Electrical, Brooke is the heartbeat of our organization. She oversees all executive, administrative, accounting, and HR operations, ensuring the business runs with precision and purpose.

Brooke provides the "big picture" direction while meticulously managing the day-to-day schedules that keep our projects on track. She wears many hats—envisionary, decision-maker, and leader—magnetizing our team around a shared goal of excellence.

B

Brad Oast

Vice President

Brad serves as our Vice President and technical authority, holding a Universal Master Electrician License in North Carolina. His journey began at age 14 as an electrician's helper, sparking a lifelong passion for the trade.

With over 20 years of hands-on experience, Brad combines technical expertise with a distinctly Southern charm that puts clients at ease. He has built and mentored a team of over 30 professionals, instilling in them the drive to "always learn more, do more, and be the best."
