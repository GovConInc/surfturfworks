Who We Are

Brad's Electrical Construction Company is a certified Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) serving commercial, industrial, and government clients across North Carolina.

Founded by the dynamic team of Brooke Oast and Brad Oast, we bring together executive leadership and hands-on field expertise to deliver electrical solutions that exceed expectations.

With projects spanning organizations like Amazon, Cummins, Siemens, Winston-Salem Government, and UNC — we've earned our reputation as North Carolina's go-to electrical contractor for complex, mission-critical work.

Brooke Oast

President & Owner

As the founder and leader of Brad's Electrical, Brooke oversees all executive, administrative, accounting, and HR operations. She provides the "big picture" vision for the business while managing day-to-day scheduling for every project.

Brooke wears many hats — she's the visionary, the decision-maker, and the person who keeps the team organized, paid, and focused on customer satisfaction. Her leadership is the magnet that draws our team together.

BO

Brad Oast

Vice President & Master Electrician

Brad holds a Universal Master Electrician License in North Carolina and brings over 20 years of hands-on experience to every project. He started as an electrician's helper at just 14 years old — and the passion never left.

His dedication to always learning more, doing more, and being the best has built a team of 30+ full-time employees ranging from master electricians to apprentices. Brad's experience, passion, and southern charm make him the electrical "guy" you want on your job.
