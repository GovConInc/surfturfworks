Our Story

"Building stable customer relationships and maintaining a stable financial system—that's what being a small company means to us."

Brad's Electrical Construction Company was founded in 2018 in Winston-Salem, NC by Bradley Oast—a master electrician with decades of hands-on experience. What started as a vision to do electrical work the right way has grown into a trusted partner for Fortune 500 manufacturing facilities, major universities like NC A&T, and government agencies.

We hold an Unlimited Electrical License (#32448-U), which means we can handle projects of any size—from a simple panel replacement to a $1M+ manufacturing facility upgrade. Our team of 20 specialized electrical professionals performs all work in-house, never relying on subcontractors. That's how we maintain our rigorous quality standards.
